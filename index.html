COOLEST CARS!!

Following a poll of some of the most informed people around the world-AutoExpres readers-here are the results: it's the ten coolest cars ever!

But what does it mean for a car to be cool? The cool cars here are the ones that took pride of place as posters on bedroom walls.

They have an ageless quality that puts them down as instant classics, but there's something more that makes them cool.

A car's coolness can be down to looks, it's achievements, the technology on board,or even the performance, there's no fixed definition of what makes a car cool.

The top ten coolest cars include:

  • Citroen DS
  • Jaguar E-Type
  • Lancia Stratos
  • Land Rover Defender
  • Lamborghini Miura
  • Mini
  • Lotus Esprit
  • Porsche 911
  • Audi Quattro
  • McLaren F1

ORIGIN OF CAR MODEL NAME

The car's model name can also be crucial to its image.

Something like Shelby Cobra conjures up connotationa of a deadly animal, although the exception to this would be Ferrari-who seem to get by just fine with mere numbers.
